최종 수정본 에세이

The Talented and Colorful Parrot

What animal is famous for its ability to talk? Parrots are! These birds live in jungles, eat nuts, fruit, and seeds. My favorite animal is the parrot because they are talented and colorful.

First, parrots are talented animals because they can mimic human speech. This is a very special skill that few other animals have. For example, if a person says "Hello" to a parrot, the parrot can copy the word exactly. Therefore, a parrot is a skilled animal.

Second, parrots are colorful, which helps them survive. Their bright feathers allow them to easily blend into the tropical rainforest. For example, the green, orange, red, and yellow colors on a parrot's body match the flowers and leaves of the jungle. For these reasons, a parrot is a beautiful and colorful animal.

In conclusion, a parrot is a great animal because it is full of color and really skilled. They can mimic human speech and blend into the tropical rainforest. If you want a talented and colorful animal, a parrot is a wonderful choice.
