The Person I Admire Most in History

Many people we admire succeeded because of their bravery and leadership. George Washington was the first president, and he fought hard to win the Revolutionary War. I admire George Washington because of his great leadership and bravery.

First of all, George Washington had great leadership. Leadership means he led people in the right way during difficult times. For instance, because of his leadership, he became the first president of the United States. This is why George Washington led America in the right way.

Secondly, George Washington was brave. Bravery means doing something even though you are scared. If he had no bravery, he couldn't have done anything. For instance, in the Revolutionary War, even though Britain blockaded Boston Harbor and was much stronger, he won because of his bravery. Thus, this event showed George Washington's bravery.

To wrap up, I admire George Washington because he had bravery and leadership. George Washington led people in the right way, and he fought in the Revolutionary War bravely. I hope one day I can be an extraordinary person like George Washington in the future.
