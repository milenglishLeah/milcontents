Albert Schweitzer

Anyone can become a respected person through their actions, and Albert Schweitzer is one of those respected people. Albert Schweitzer was a German doctor who went to Africa to help African people. I admire Albert Schweitzer because he was hardworking and determined to help others.

One reason I admire Albert Schweitzer is that he was so helpful. He cured many poor African people and established hospitals. The second reason I admire Albert Schweitzer is because he was so determined. He never gave up on saving African people, and he was willing to sacrifice himself for them. For instance, he gave up a stable job in Germany and went to Gabon to care for the sick. Even though some African people didn't believe in him at first, he didn't care and kept curing the sick. He only concentrated on his mission. As a result, his determination and spirit of sacrifice contributed to improving the quality of life for African people.

In conclusion, Albert Schweitzer is the person I admire due to his dedication and determination. He saved many poor African people by curing the sick, establishing hospitals, and even sacrificing his own comfort. In the future, I wish I could be a heroic person like Albert Schweitzer.
