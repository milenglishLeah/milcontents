파트 D: 최종 수정본 에세이

What Makes a Friendship Last

Some friendships seem to last perpetually, free from quarrels that could aggravate the relationship. A lasting friendship is built on a foundation of positive actions. To make friendships last, we need to communicate tenderly, maintain a sense of balance and fairness, and show mutual respect for our friends.

First, communicating kindly is essential for a strong friendship. Using appropriate and mild words makes others feel comfortable and encourages them to respond in a positive way. For example, when I use gentle words with my friends, I feel that we become closer. On the other hand, speaking angrily can astonish and hurt others. The famous scientists Marie Curie and Albert Einstein, for instance, maintained their professional relationship by exchanging letters respectfully. If they had spoken with unruly voices, their collaborative spirit could have been contaminated. Therefore, communicating with good manners helps build a stronger and more joyful friendship.

Second, a lasting friendship requires balance and fairness. When you are willing to share what you have, friends will want to be with you. For example, when a friend visited my home, I shared my snacks and my gaming console, which made him happy and strengthened our bond. It is also important to be fair to avoid causing your friends distress. For instance, if friends are playing a game together, they should take turns and follow the rules equally. If one person always insists on going first or changes the rules to win, it feels unfair and can damage the friendship. In the same way, sharing with friends and being fair in your interactions helps bring you closer together.

Third, we must show mutual respect to make the rapport with friends stronger. This begins with trusting what others say. For example, I have learned that trusting my friends reduces my stress, whereas doubting them creates it. It is also critical to respect a friend's personal boundaries. For instance, trying to know a friend's private thoughts can be a violation of their boundaries and cause them to be upset. Therefore, trust and a respect for boundaries are the core components of mutual respect.

To sum up, to make friendships last perpetually, we must communicate kindly, be balanced and fair, and respect our friends mutually. To maintain these relationships, we have to perpetually care for our friends and avoid drastic arguments. We also need to speak in a considerate way so that others can feel comfortable and valued.

에세이의 가장 큰 강점은 명확한 구조입니다. 서론에서 세 가지 이유를 제시하고, 각 본론에서 그 이유를 하나씩 설명하는 방식은 매우 논리적이고 설득력 있는 글쓰기의 기본이에요. 이 기본기를 잘 이해하고 있다는 점이 정말 대단합니다.

한 단계 더 성장하기 위한 제안: 지금 학생의 가장 큰 과제는 정확성을 높이는 것입니다. 좋은 아이디어와 멋진 단어들을 가지고 있지만, 문법과 단어 형태의 오류 때문에 그 생각이 100% 전달되지 못하는 점이 아쉬워요.

  • 1. 단어 형태 확인하기: `balanceful`이나 `rejoiceful`처럼 직접 단어를 만들기보다, 사전을 통해 `balanced`나 `joyful`과 같은 정확한 형태를 찾아보는 연습을 해보세요.
