최종 수정본 에세이

Neil Armstrong: A Hero of Bravery and Leadership

A person can be respected and change the world by having good leadership. Neil Armstrong is one of the people who are greatly respected. He was an American astronaut who became the first person to walk on the moon. I admire Neil Armstrong because he was brave and had humble and quiet leadership.

To begin with, I admire Neil Armstrong because he was brave. He was the first person to go to the moon, and at the time, no one had gone before, so people didn't know if it was safe. As an illustration, he rode the Apollo 11 spacecraft to the moon on July 16, 1969. This required immense courage. Therefore, people still admire Neil Armstrong because he was brave.

Secondly, Neil Armstrong was humble and had quiet leadership. Even when other astronauts were in a panic because the lunar module had an error, he stayed calm and took manual control. For example, when a critical switch broke, he used a simple pen to push the circuit breaker back in, allowing the team to lift off from the moon. Therefore, Neil Armstrong's leadership helped his team land safely and return home.

To conclude, Neil Armstrong is the person I admire due to his heroic actions. He was the first person to go to the moon, and he was calm even when there was an emergency. I hope I can accomplish an extraordinary deed to influence other people in the future, like Neil Armstrong.
